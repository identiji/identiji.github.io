Quick Start Guide

Why would we want to use passwords with emojis?

They are:

• Easy for us to remember

• Extra hard for bots to guess

What makes a strong emoji-infused password?

  1. Use a mix of emojis and common words.

  2. Place the emojis and common words as randomly as possible.
